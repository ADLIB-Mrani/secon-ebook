### What is an E-book?

An electronic book (e-book) is a digital publication that can be read on computers, tablets, smartphones, or dedicated e-reading devices. E-books have revolutionized the publishing industry by making content more accessible and affordable.

## Chapter 5: Best Practices

### Content Quality

✅ Use well-formatted source content
✅ Verify URLs before scraping
✅ Organize content logically
✅ Include proper attribution

❌ Don't use copyrighted material without permission
❌ Avoid low-quality or duplicate content
❌ Don't include broken links

### Design Tips

- Choose templates that match your content type
- Use consistent formatting throughout
- Include a table of contents for long books
- Add chapter breaks for better reading experience

### Performance

- Limit file sizes for faster processing
- Use local files when possible
- Batch similar content together
- Test with small samples first
